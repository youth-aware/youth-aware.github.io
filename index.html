About Us

YouthAware was founded with the aim of improving access to holistic education in India. Our primary goal is to spread awareness and propagate innovation to solve challenges faced by society. YouthAware will hold seminars to destigmatize topics related to mental health and well being, an essential topic for today's youth. We will also organize sessions with successful changemakers to provide an insight into their journey from ideation to action. YouthAware will conduct competitions to promote innovation and support new and exciting initiatives. We will use avenues like social media and online events to raise funds for under-resourced schools. With the aim of delivering quality holistic education and achieving the maximum potential of each student, the funds will be used to promote out of the box thinking to inspire students to make a meaningful impact in their communities.

Education is the perfect tool to catalyse large scale impact in the world. However the current education system in India focuses on accumulation of theoretical knowledge which dampens innovation and practical problem solving skills. This rote learning based education format creates a systemic failure in the quest to work towards the Sustainable Development Goals. If students are equipped with the tools to make micro-improvements in their communities, the result is a large-scale positive impact in the world. Increasing awareness of the biggest challenges our generation faces, challenging students to innovate pragmatic solutions, and upholding and incubating novel ideas to completion should be integral features of our education system, thereby enabling a sustainable future. We want to tap into the tremendous underutilized potential of the youth and foster the spirit of positive change making in them.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is YouthAware?

    Founded by a team of high school students, YouthAware is a not-for-profit organization with the aim of helping the youth in holistic development.

  • What is our mission?

    Our mission is to reach out to as many people as possible and help spread awareness on issues that affect any and everyone . We believe it is extremely important that the right information is spread as we are aware of the consequences of feeding false information on the ever growing minds of the youth. Hence, we have set out on a mission to have a positive impact on society by reaching out to people who nobody has before while remaining the most credible source out there.

  • How we plan on achieving our goals and mission?

    We believe that the best way to achieve our goal is to directly interact with as many people as possible. In Covid times, we plan on doing this by holding regular online seminars on different social media platforms. However, after Covid, we will also be conducting live seminars is different locations to reach people belonging to all type of backgrounds . We also have a strong social media presence as we know it is the best and most effective way to connect to the youth.

  • How are we unique?

    Our organisation is run by the youth therefore we understand how to connect and reach out to the youth is the most effective ways. We also understand how important it is to spread the correct information and the gravity of the consequences that come with it. Therefore we are unique as we are not only trying to reach out to as many people as possible, we are doing it in the most effective ways while being the most credible source.

  • What are the topics we usually cover and why are they important?

    As a relatively new organisation, we only have limited resources which only allows us to cover few topics but as we expand, we intent to brand out to as many genres as possible to have as vast an impact as possible. As of now, we typically go over topics like cyberbullying, substance abuse and mental heath. These topics have been picked by us as we are aware how they are affecting the youth and because not enough light is being brought to them.
