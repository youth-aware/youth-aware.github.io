About Us

YouthAware was founded with the aim of improving access to holistic education in India. Our primary goal is to spread awareness and propagate innovation to solve challenges faced by society. YouthAware will hold seminars to destigmatize topics related to mental health and well being, an essential topic for today's youth. We will also organize sessions with successful changemakers to provide an insight into their journey from ideation to action. YouthAware will conduct competitions to promote innovation and support new and exciting initiatives. We will use avenues like social media and online events to raise funds for under-resourced schools. With the aim of delivering quality holistic education and achieving the maximum potential of each student, the funds will be used to promote out of the box thinking to inspire students to make a meaningful impact in their communities.

Education is the perfect tool to catalyse large scale impact in the world. However the current education system in India focuses on accumulation of theoretical knowledge which dampens innovation and practical problem solving skills. This rote learning based education format creates a systemic failure in the quest to work towards the Sustainable Development Goals. If students are equipped with the tools to make micro-improvements in their communities, the result is a large-scale positive impact in the world. Increasing awareness of the biggest challenges our generation faces, challenging students to innovate pragmatic solutions, and upholding and incubating novel ideas to completion should be integral features of our education system, thereby enabling a sustainable future. We want to tap into the tremendous underutilized potential of the youth and foster the spirit of positive change making in them.
